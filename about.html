Hey, I'm Jeffy

I make serious ideas fun - and fun ideas serious.

I am a builder, storyteller, startup founder, and professional dot-connector who believes curiosity is a competitive advantage. I spend my days (and far too many nights) at the intersection of innovation, community, technology, and "what if?"

JeffyMakesItFun (JMIF) is where I explore those intersections out loud - sometimes thoughtfully, sometimes playfully, always honestly.

If you're into innovation with personality, civic pride without fluff, and ideas that don't take themselves too seriously, you're in the right place.

My Origin Story (aka Why This Exists)

I've always been obsessed with how ideas move - from spark to thing that actually exists in the world.

That obsession led me to:

  • Build Startup(s)
  • Work at an Innovation Hub
  • Experiment Relentlessly with Tech
  • Champion Underdog Regions + Overlooked Communities
  • Ask Annoying-but-Necessary Questions
  • Inspire Others with Fun Storytelling
  • Launch This Blog

Illinois - especially Greater Peoria - has building in our lifeblood. The region is full of dreamers, doers, and deliverers. We've just been historically bad at telling our own story. This right here - it's my little nudge in changing that.

What Drives Me

I am motivated by a few core beliefs:

  • Innovation isn't Just for Silicon Valley | Big ideas belong everywhere - especially in the Midwest
  • Technology Should Feel Human | If it's powerful but soulless, we're doing it wrong.
  • Community is the Ultimate Force Multiplier | Cool things happen way faster when people actually take the time to talk to each other.
  • Fun is Not the Opposite of Serious | Fun is how serious ideas survive long enough to matter.

JMIF exists to explore all of this and more - without pretending everything is already figured out.

Why I Write

Writing is how I think. Writing is how I express big ideas - and tiny ones. It's how I:

  • Process what I'm building
  • Make sense of what I'm learning
  • Share what's working (and what absolutely is not)
  • Turn half-formed ideas into something useful

This site is part idea lab, part public notebook, part storytelling engine. Some posts are going to be polished. Some are experiments. All of them... honest.

If something sparks a conversation, challenges an assumption or makes you say "huh... I hadn't thought about it that way," then JMIF is doing its job.

What You'll Find Here:

I can't do rigid lanes - my brain will never work that way. But here's the general terrain:

  • Illinovation | Innovation stories rooted in Illinois - past, present, and future.
  • Inside Distillery Labs | What's actually happening inside an innovation hub
  • AI, But Make it Understandable | Interesting, practical, and occasionally weird uses of AI - no hype, no jargon

  • Generative AI Experiments | Playing with tools, prompts, visuals, workflows, and ideas - sometimes just to see if it can be broken.

  • Food, Travel + Exploration | Because curiosity doesn't only live in the office.
  • Useless (but Delightful) Trivia | No justification needed.
  • Whatever Else my ADHD Decides is Fascinating | You've been warned.

Credibility, Minus the Ego

I'm not writing from the sidelines. I'm not an armchair founder. I am actively:

  • Building a Startup
  • Working with Founders, Students, and Innovators
  • Experimenting with Emerging Tech
  • Supporting the Local Ecosystem
  • Building Programs to Create a New Generation of Entrepreneurs
  • Supporting More Women In Startups
  • Advocating for More Inclusive, Human-Centered Innovation

This space isn't just theory - it's coming from lived experience, tested ideas, and honest reflection over wins and brutal failures.
